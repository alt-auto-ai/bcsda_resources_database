4 FSG’s key messages (1) • Forests can be carbon neutral. • Wood can be carbon neutral. • Forests with stable or increasing carbon stocks, produce carbon neutral wood. • Biogenic carbon in products and fuels made from carbon neutral wood and biomass is carbon neutral.
