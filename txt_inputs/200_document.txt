IV Acknowledgments: Financial support for research is provided by the China Council for International Cooperation on Environment and Development (CCICED), who also convened the Task Force. The Support from CCICED has enabled sufficient discussion, communication, and surveys among experts from China and internationally, which have served as the essential foundation of the research work. Special thanks to the Chief Advisers of CCICED Advisory Committee, Professor SHEN Guofang and Dr. Art Hanson, Leader of Chief Advisers‟ Support Team Dr. Ren Yong , and the CCICED Secretariat and support office in providing information and organization and coordination support . The Task Force extends cordial thanks to the CCICED. As part of the research process, eight discussion forums were held including three in Sweden, the United Kingdom, and Norway. Thanks to the hosts for organizing visits to local demonstration projects on low - carbon economy, relevant research units and government departments. Also thanks to the British Embassy in China who organized a small seminar following the completion of the first draft of the report to ensure expert inputs. We are honored to receive comments from Lord Nicolas Stern of the London School of Economics, Su Ming, deputy director of Research Institute of Fiscal Science of the Ministry of Finance, Professor He Jiankun and Professor Zhang Kunmin from Tsinghua University, Professor Cui Dapeng from the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, and Tang Min, Deputy Secretary - General of China Development Research Fou ndation.
