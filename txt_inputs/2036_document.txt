Annex I Regulation for the Prevention of Pollution by Oil September 2003 Covers oil pollution from operations and accidents.1992 amendments mandated double hulls for new oil tankers and a phased retrofit for existing ones. October 1983 Issues detailed standards on packing, marking, labeling , documentation, stowage, quantity limitations, exceptions, and notifications. July 1992 Annex II Regulations for the Control of Pollution by Noxious Liquid Substances in Bulk Discharge criteria and measures for control of noxious liquid substances carried in bulk. Annex III Prevention of Pollution by Harmful Substances Carried by Sea in Packaged Form Annex IV Prevention of Pollution by Sewage from Ships Sets requirements to control sea pollution by sewage, allowing discharge only if treated by approved systems or at specified distances from land. Annex V Prevention of Pollution by Garbage from Ships Sets requirements for how and where garbage can be disposed of. All plastic disposal banned. December 1988 May 2005 Annex VI Prevention of Air Pollution from Ships Limits sulfur and nitrogen oxide onboard emissions, bans ozone- depleting substances. Energy efficiency measures adopted 2011.
