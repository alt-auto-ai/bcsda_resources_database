5 December 2012 BET Module 4: Managing and mitigating impacts Introduction to the course Audience The audience will have an environmental and sustainability background, but a background in environmental economics is not necessary. Delegates could include:  Sustainability managers  CSR managers  EHS managers  Lifecycle analysis professionals  Operations managers, or  Those with a background of integration social performance and investment Please note this list is not exhaustive. This module is suitable for participants who have completed modules 1, 2 and 3. It provides an introduction to the different policy mechanisms that may be used to address their ecosystems impact. The course may be conducted as internal training or an external course for delegates from a number of companies, therefore this module will be an opportunity for delegates to understand how different companies or departments are currently accounting for biodiversity and ecosystem services.
