1 WBCSD F orest Solutions G roup: L ist of K ey P erformance Indicators List of Key Performance Indicators WBCSD Forest Solutions Group is the global platform where leading business in the forest products sector build and share solutions to sustainable development. FSG’s mission is to grow an inclusive circular bioeconomy that is rooted in thriving working forests. As a sign of shared commitment, members of FSG adhere to Membership Principles & Responsibilities, and report annually on a set of KPIs to track progress on the commitments made in the Forest Sector SDG Roadmap.
