Companies are facing increasing pressure from regulators, investors, consumers and other key stakeholders to integrate sustainability in all aspects of their business. In other words, sustainability is now mainstream, and continued education at all levels of a company is central to ensuring the continued success of business as the world transitions towards a net-zero, nature-positive and equitable future. The WBCSD Leadership Program educates all business functions on sustainable development challenges and opportunities that feed into strategic business decision-making. As the world continues to change, sustainable businesses will grow to be the most successful. Prof Dr Rodney Irwin Chief Operating Officer Foreword Therefore, it’s critical for the next generation of leaders to understand the importance of sustainability and integrate related challenges and opportunities into their core business strategy. The Leadership Program has over 20 years of excellence in providing representatives from nearly 150 companies with cutting-edge research into the sustainability agenda. It has shared insights into how leading companies use innovation to develop value-creating solutions that address sustainability issues and opportunities. We are always inspired by the innovative ideas, concrete solutions, and lasting relationships from the impassioned, fearless business leaders who participate. WBCSD is proud to deliver this curriculum in partnership with Yale University and IMD, as well as in collaboration with our member companies, who represent the world’s most forward-thinking businesses driving accelerated progress in the sustainability agenda. Business resilience requires sustainability leadership to come from all business functions. We welcome you to join a dynamic community of business leaders committed to realizing a world where everyone can live healthy, fulfilling lives within planetary boundaries. We look forward to meeting you in this learning journey and working with you to translate your ideas into action. Suzanne Feinmann Director, Education WBCSD Leadership Program | 2

Sustainability leadership skills: Participants gain the latest insights into sustainable development and a better understanding of their own leadership preferences to help them become sustainability leaders. Academic certification from Yale and IMD: We conduct our Program at Yale University and IMD w ith world-renowned academics. Professors who have contributed in previous years include David Bach, Julia Binder, Peter Boyd , Zoë Chance, Dan Esty , Omar Toulan and Julie Zimmerman . After successful completion of the Program, participants receive certificates from Yale School of Management and I MD. A strong network: The Program creates a global network of dynamic business leaders, capable of acting as sustainable development ambassadors within their companies and in society. There are also opportunities to engage with a range of business and sustainability experts. What participants gain by joining Strengthen ed business skills: Participants work on individual and group projects focusing on solutions that bene f it the participant’s company and the wider business community. Final group projects are presented during WBCSD’s Council Meeting, where participants interact with WBCSD members at the C-suite level. Varied experiences: The Program uses a highly interactive mixture of education styles from traditional classroom-based work to action and experiential learning activities through workshop participation, case studies and presentations to help candidates to succeed in their career.
