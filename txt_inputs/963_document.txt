Acknowledgement We would like to thank The Energy and Resources Institute (TERI) for preparing this report. We would also like to thank Toyota Motor Corporation and Renault S.A. for their support in preparing this case study and for their invaluable contribution in organizing the stakeholder dialogue in Bangalore. We also deeply appreciate the valuable input we received from the participants who took part in the stakeholder dialogue in Bangalore. Disclaimer This case study has been prepared by The Energy and Resources Institute (TERI) with input from several member companies as a part of the Mobility for Development (M4D) project at the World Business Council for Sustainable Development (WBCSD). It includes views and opinions from a stakeholder dialogue that was held in Bangalore. This does not mean, however, that every WBCSD member company agrees with every word.
