Future from Fibre From Forest to Finished Product 5 Preface This technical report has a twofold origin: the WWF’s “Living Forests” campaign/ report and WBCSD activities in the same area – which found each other. Likewise, the streams of Pöyry and IIASA work in this area also found each other here. From initial WWF/WBCSD collaboration and discussions, we have as an outcome this look at forest and new forest products up to 2050. The report, commissioned by the WBCSD Forest Solutions Group in cooperation with WWF International, is based on work by Pöyry Management Consulting Oy (Pöyry), and written by Petri Vasara and Hannele Lehtinen from Pöyry.
