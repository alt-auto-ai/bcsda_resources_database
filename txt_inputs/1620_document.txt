3 WBCSD EDUCATION → Learning for impact World Business Council for Sustainable Development Foreword Sustainability has transitioned from a peripheral concern in business to a central force driving strategic decisions and transformative actions. As the world grapples with the challenges posed by climate emergency, nature loss, and mounting inequality, the need for businesses to embrace sustainability has never been more critical and expected by stakeholders ranging from regulators to consumers. Indeed, the world is turning to business for sustainable solutions. To support this ambition, the World Business Council for Sustainable Development (WBCSD) and its members are committed to accelerating the transformation of businesses and their value chains. Beyond the rhetoric of "why engage," our focus is on the practical "how to" of operationalizing business transformation for a more sustainable future. Crucially, we aim to highlight the vital role of education and training in driving this transformation. Research by LinkedIn shows demand for sustainability skills outpacing supply by a rate of two to one in 2024. For businesses to navigate the sustainability transformation effectively, leaders must take action to bridge this emerging gap by equipping their people with the skills, knowledge and tools to effect change. For over two decades, WBCSD Education has worked with subject matter experts and leading academics, providing the necessary training and insights to help business leaders navigate the complexities of sustainability. The success of our executive education programs stems from our philosophy of ensuring business-relevant, meaningful and actionable learning, coupled with strong partnerships with renowned academic institutions, including Yale, ESADE, IMD and Stanford. WBCSD Education is also continuing to expand its offering to meet the growing demand for sustainability education and training in the business community. Through bespoke programs, digital learning and collaboration with leading organizations and universities, we aim to empower a new generation of leaders who can drive change within their organizations and contribute to a sustainable and equitable world. This brochure is not just a collection of information; it is a testament to our commitment to build up the business talent and skills needed for a net-zero, nature-positive and equitable future. Join us on our transformative learning journeys to redefine the future of business. 4 WBCSD EDUCATION → Learning for impact World Business Council for Sustainable Development Our Approach The mission of WBCSD Education is to provide business leaders and practitioners with relevant, actionable and meaningful learning courses and materials to raise ambition to transform, drive collaborative action and sharpen accountability around sustainability goals. We envision a world in which sustainability is embedded within mainstream business and executive education, training and learning. Our north star is to build up the business talent and skills needed for a net-zero, nature-positive and equitable future. In support of this vision, WBCSD Education seeks to deliver learning solutions to the global business community that are pragmatic, credible, agile, and designed for business by business. We also endeavor to employ a human-centric approach designing curricula by putting ourselves in the shoes of different groups of learners, to ensure that our learning formats, experiences and services are engaging, interactive and purpose- driven. As part of these efforts, we currently deliver education services across three key pillars of activity: Executive education A program for every stage of your learning journey - from immersive c-suite experiences to early career development. Bespoke programs Co-designed solutions tailored to support your organization’s unique sustainability challenges and opportunities. Self-paced digital learning Sustainability learning at scale. Access to an expanding library of online courses across nature, climate, and equity.
