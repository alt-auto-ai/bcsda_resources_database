@wbcsd WBCSD AGM 2017 The WBCSD Annual General Meeting was held Tuesday 17 October 2017 at the Hotel Hilton Reforma, Mexico City, Mexico. The AGM was attended by 37 Council Members. 19 Council Members provided their proxy vote to the Chairman of the meeting, WBCSD Chairman and CEO of Unilever, Paul Polman.
