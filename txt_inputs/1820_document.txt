C o m m u n i t i e s P e o p l e P r o c u r e m e n t C l i m a t e W a t e r C i r c u l a r i t y W O R K I N G F O R E S T S B I O - E C O N O M Y Context The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) present business with the opportunity to demonstrate leadership, apply its knowledge and creativity and provide innovative solutions. Industry peers and value chain partners can leverage the universal framework of the SDGs to inform their actions, identify new forms of collaboration, unlock investment opportunities and accelerate the transition to 2030. Project overview Through the Forest Sector SDG Roadmap, the Forest Solutions Group (FSG) of the World Business Council for Sustainable Development (WBCSD) seeks to work collaboratively with its sector peers and stakeholders to help the forest sector navigate the challenges that lie ahead and ensure that the industry continues to grow and thrive in line with the SDGs. The FSG is the global platform for the forest sector value chain to build and share business solutions to lead sustainable development in the forest products sector. Its mission is to advance the bio-economy and a thriving forest sector that sustains healthy productive forests and people’s well-being. Approach The Roadmap articulates a joint vision of the most impactful contributions the forest sector can make to the SDGs through process, product and partnership innovation. It describes the sector’s current level of alignment with the SDGs and identifies key impact opportunities and actions for the most material SDGs and targets. Options for engagement: The Roadmap seeks to guide, inform and influence decision- making and actions along the forest products value chain and foster stakeholder collaboration to amplify SDG impact. • Explore the Roadmap in more detail and learn more about concrete business action. • Be part of the dialogue and action through the Forest Solutions Group.
