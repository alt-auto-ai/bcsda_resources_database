The MPGCA is the mandated process in which national governments work with Non-Party Stakeholders , including business, on global climate action. Evening Solutions Dialogue: Financing the transition to a regenerative food system DATE: 1 Dec TIME: 19:00 - 22:00 ZONE: Green Zone THEME: F&A PARTNERS: NOW Partners (OP2B) P rogramme Overview: WBCSD at COP 28 Invitation Only At the end of a long and intense day at COP you will be able to stroll from the Blue Zone to the beautiful Terra Pavilion’s auditorium and roof terrace, located just a few steps away in the Green Zone. This evening Solutions Dialogue Series brings together key leaders of the food and fiber system and related sector companies, finance and investment, politics, policy, farmer movements and other stakeholder groups. Together we will consider how to finance the transition towards a regenerative food system together. This evening event is organized by the Future Economy Forum and co-hosted by One PlanetBusiness for Biodiversity (OP2B) and the World Business Council for Sustainable Development (WBCSD) and the sponsors of the Solutions Dialogue Series, in the spirit of an Emirati majlis, fostering partnership and deep human relationship that lead to concrete outcomes. Context Transitioning to regenerative and resilient agricultural systems is widely recognized as one of the key solutions to reverse climate change and biodiversity loss. Evidence has shown that these changes are underway – and millions of farmers are at the forefront. However, the risks and cost of the transition are on the farmer’s shoulders. In order to transition to more resilient and regenerative models, farmers need CAPEX, OPEX, cash flow and risk management. Agricultural value chain players, already committed to supporting the transition, are therefore calling for funds to achieve the transition at scale. We present a call to action for the global finance community to unlock investments in food systems transformation, including public-private partnerships. Solutions Dialogue Series at COP28 This evening will serve as the kickoff for the COP28 event series that starts on December 1st and continues at the Terra Pavilion on December 8th and 10th. In addition, we will hold, throughout COP28, smaller working group sessions on key topics in the Blue Zone pavilions of the Future Economy Forum and the Food Systems Pavilion. 1 Dec
