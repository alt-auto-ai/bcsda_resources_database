Saraburi Sandbox , Thailand's blueprint for its first low-carbon city, is driven by a Public-Private-People Partnership model. It exploits an area-based approach to modernize policy and regulation to govern the transformation across sector in line with Nationally Determined Contribution (NDC) while seeking the international support of green finances for low carbon technology and green infrastructure. This initiative aims to transform industries and city into a green and low-carbon economy, fostering an improved quality of life for its residents and stimulating economic growth while ensuring no one is left behind during the transition. Key initiatives include a pilot energy transition project for selling electricity via Third-Party Access (TPA) to reduce costs and promote renewable energy use, turning waste into value as raw materials or alternative energy sources such as bio-coal, biogas, alternative fuels, and etc., and promoting low-carbon agriculture with climate-friendly rice production using the Alternate Wetting and Drying (AWD) process and Napier grass as a source of renewable fuel. The sandbox also emphasizes increasing green spaces to enhance carbon sink capacity, exploring carbon capture, utilization, and storage (CCUS) technology, using low-carbon cement in the construction value chain, and driving innovation. Green City Green Industry SCG is advancing the transition to comprehensive clean energy through innovative solutions like Smart Grid innovations. This smart network efficiently manages clean energy, ensuring accessibility at all times. Additionally, future innovations such as batteries that store thermal energy from clean sources aim to replace fossil fuels in heating and steam production processes. By driving such advancements, SCG is committed to fostering a sustainable future. Accelerating Green Innovation in the Decarbonization Journey SCG is driving green innovations, propelling the company towards its decarbonization goals. Embracing the concept of 'Inclusive Green Growth,' SCG fosters the development of sustainable solutions with a clear vision: to build a green and low-carbon society that enhances the quality of life, stimulates sustainable economic growth, and ensures a healthy environment. SCG is transforming the construction materials sector with green and smart living solutions, emphasizing the development of environmentally friendly technologies. By introducing Thailand's first Low Carbon Cement, which uses less clinker to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, along with various low-carbon materials, showcases their commitment to sustainability. These sustainable materials can be applied to a wide range of eco-friendly projects, from large-scale buildings to industrial estates. Green Solution & Smart Living SCG is committed to endlessly utilizing used resources within a circular economy. Innovations like high-quality SCGC GREEN POLYMER TM which enhance recycling efficiency and reduce carbon emissions from virgin plastic consumption. Additionally, SCGP offers sustainable packaging designed to reduce resource use and enhance durability while meeting all application requirements. Green Circularity SCGJWD leads in green logistics by collaborating with stakeholders to reduce GHG Scope 3 emissions. By employing advanced technologies such as electric vehicles, AI-driven route optimization, and Automated Conveyor Robots (AGV & Robotics), SCGJWD enhances transportation efficiency and sustainability. Their Green Logistics Solutions utilize digital technologies to manage transportation networks across ASEAN, promoting clean energy and environmentally friendly warehousing.
