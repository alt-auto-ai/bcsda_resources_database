0 To join the COP28 Action Agenda on Regenerative Landscapes, the following participation criteria apply: For more information, for any questions you might have and to access the survey, please reach out to us via email . 1. Guiding principles: Support the UN Global Compact Ten Principles and follow the Food and Business Principles, acknowledging that any violation will result in removal from this initiative 2a. Accountability: Willing to report against key impact areas relevant for regenerative agriculture and landscapes, including but not limited to soil health, GHG emissions, biodiversity, water, farmer livelihoods, leveraging established science-based reporting frameworks, tools, and metrics 2b. Accountability: Willing to consider following increasingly holistic approaches encompassing environmental, social, economic impact areas and be guided by independently developed frameworks and tools once published 3. Survey: Submit complete survey data on existing and new, global and landscape-level regenerative agriculture commitments and initiatives; engage to refine the data submitted; and agree to update annually 4. Data sharing and protection: Agree for the data shared via the survey to be included, at an aggregate level, in communications and in public platforms to support transparency, awareness and matchmaking Participation criteria
