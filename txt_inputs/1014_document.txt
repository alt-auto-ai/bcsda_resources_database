OP2B / Anthesis 5 Process & project overview PROJECT AIM: • To research and assess the different funding mechanisms available across Europe that can support a wide scale transition to regenerative agriculture, through a 360 degrees, high-level lens. This is aimed at supporting a pilot programme of approximately 50,000 hectares. PROCESS: Phase 1: discover Phase 2: assess Desk-based research Benchmarking Stakeholder interviews Scoring Ranking, depending on the overall score for each funding opportunity
