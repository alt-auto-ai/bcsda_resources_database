SAP Last April, SAP established a €3M COVID-19 Emergency Fund to support organizations on the front lines, including the World Health Organization and CDC Foundation. Last week SAP doubled its commitment by adding another €3M for a total of €6M to the COVID-19 Emergency Fund with a focus on India. For this second phase commitment, SAP will donate €1M to COVAX , an effort co-led by the Coalition for Epidemic Preparedness Innovations (CEPI), Gavi, the World Health Organization (WHO), and long-term SAP partner UNICEF, to accelerate fair and equitable access to vaccines for every country in the world. Beyond that donation, we will use the emergency fund to support India- based employees, their families, and the communities in which they live and work. This includes facilitating vaccinations to employees and their families at our SAP Labs India facilities and augmenting public health care systems with the aim to build additional provisions of oxygen supplies and ventilators.
