WBCSD Key messages around COVID-19 3 April 2020 The health and well-being of WBCSD members, partners and staff is of utmost importance to us. around COVID-19 WBCSD key messages As an organization, we want to remain relevant, engaging credible and solutions-focused – and we are rallying the WBCSD team across the world to engage our network of members & partners – with a spirit of adaptability, determination and resilience. It is crucial to support each other , work together and do what business does best: respond fast. It is equally essential to continue our work on sustainable development. The world needs immediate business actions to address the crisis and shape the future. This is a pivotal moment for each company to demonstrate responsibility towards society by deploying all its care, critical know-how, reach and resources. WBCSD has both the responsibility and the opportunity to act at this critical point of time. WBCSD’s call to action to leverage our combined business expertise is centered on 3 areas: 1. Vital Supply Chains: with focus on short-term supply chain resilience plan, starting with the food system; 2. Return to “Normal” Scenarios: with focus on employee health and business recovery; 3. Long-term impacts: with focus on COVID-19 vulnerabilities revealed by the crisis & lessons for future resilience and stakeholder capitalism. But we need more if the world is to respond to and learn from this crisis. Times like these demand bold leadership and action at a time in which our collective commitment to stakeholder capitalism is being put to a test. WBCSD is stepping up to the challenge to support our member companies by launching 4 a Response Program to mobilize business action at scale in response to the COVID-19 crisis.
