4 This report was undertaken by the World Business Council for Sustainable Development (WBCSD) and its Just Rural Transition Investment Partnerships workstream, and the Sustainable Rice Platform (SRP), with technical input from the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO). The report was developed as part of the work of the Sustainable Rice Landscapes Initiative (SRLI), whose founders and consortium partners include FAO, WBCSD, SRP, the International Rice Research Institute (IRRI), Deutsche Gesellschaft für Internationale Zusammenarbeit (GIZ) GmbH, and the UN Environment Program (UNEP). This report was produced by Tanja Havemann (Clarmondial) with the support of Christine Negra (Versant Vision). Clarmondial is an independent advisory firm that helps mobilize capital for sustainable natural resource management, specifically agriculture, through blended finance mechanisms and carbon finance. More information can be found at www.clarmondial.com. Additional input, guidance and review was provided by Beau Damen (FAO Regional Office for Asia and the Pacific), Victoria Crawford (WBCSD) and Wyn Ellis (SRP). The report was edited by Chris Dickson. The report was based on stakeholder interviews, a literature review, and five regional roundtables undertaken between January and September 2021.
