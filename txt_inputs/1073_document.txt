Please contact Mark Bushell at mark.bushell@ey.com. Usage instructions The logo consists of the EY letters, yellow Beam device and the tagline, Building a better working world . The EY logo is rendered in a specific configuration and should not be altered in any way other than resizing proportionately. Under no circumstances should other logos be developed. For guidance on which logo is suitable for your application, see EY’s visual identity principles available on The Branding Zone . If you require special assistance in choosing a logo to meet your particular needs, please contact TheBrandingZone@ey.com . Color The logo must always appear in EY Off Black with the Beam in EY Yellow. If being used against a dark background it should appear in white with the Beam in EY Yellow. We recommend that the color chosen should be of the highest contrast possible against the background onto which it is placed. Clear space When using the logo, a certain amount of clear space must surround it to maintain its visual integrity. No other elements may intrude on the clear space. The clear space is based on the height of the letter ‘E’ within the logo. It is preferred that no graphic element be placed directly above, below or adjacent to the logo. The logo should be used only as stated above. All other uses of the logo should follow the standards as detailed on The Branding Zone. With questions, please write to TheBrandingZone@ey.com.
