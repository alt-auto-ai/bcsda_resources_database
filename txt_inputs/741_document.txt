Communication and Stakeholder Involvement Guidebook B A T T E L L E & E R M Page ii Acknowledgements This report was prepared by Battelle Memorial Institute and Environmental Resources Management (ERM) Ltd, the managers of Substudy 1 -- Assessing Effective Stakeholder Dialogue Approaches and Substudy 2 -- Stakeholder Communication Approaches respectively, as part of the project Toward a Sustainable Cement Industry . The Sustainable Cement project is sponsored by the World Business Council for Sustainable Development and a consortium of international cement companies. The authors wish to thank the substudy champions for their detailed guidance and review: Noel Morrin of RMC Group p.l.c. (RMC), Julio Cesar M. Rocha of Votorantim, and Miguel Gonzalez of CEMEX. Special thanks to the cement company communications staff who shared their insights and experiences and connected the team with other staff and stakeholders around the world to interview. Communications staff of WGC member corporations who specifically need to be thanked for their thoughtful comments are Anne Gambling of Holcim and Paulo Rocha of CIMPOR. Also, the authors wish to extend a sincere thanks to the many stakeholders in Brazil, Thailand, Portugal, and Egypt for their time attending dialogue sessions to share their perceptions of and suggestions for the industry. Without all of these contributions, this Guidebook would not be as detailed and relevant to the cement industry. Ms. Gretchen Hund (Battelle) was the Substudy 1 manager and lead author; co-authors include Jill Engel-Cox, Kim Fowler, and Todd Peterson of Battelle, and Sarah Selby (Substudy 2 manager) and Matt Haddon of ERM.
