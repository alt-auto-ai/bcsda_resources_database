WBCSD GLOBAL NETWORK BROCHURE World Business Council for Sustainable Development A unique alliance of some 60 organizations worldwide The WBCSD Global Network is a unique alliance of more than 60 independent business-led organizations worldwide. The Global Network represents some 6’500 businesses, all united by a shared commitment to provide business leadership for sustainable development across the world. To accelerate the required transformation of businesses, their value chains and the systems in which they operate, the Global Network is a valuable ally in our efforts to catalyze business action at the speed and scale required to limit the impact of the climate crisis, restore nature and tackle inequality. A global community of sustainability leaders As a global community, the Global Network connects sustainability leaders from business, policy and civil society to exchange knowledge and insights, build capacity, and enhance each other’s reach, impact and credibility. Together, WBCSD and the Global Network are uniquely positioned to strengthen sustainability competence, advance and implement leading sustainability practices, convene meaningful partnerships and accelerate and amplify business action on sustainable development worldwide.
