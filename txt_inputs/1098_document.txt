Opening plenary This marks the formal start of the conference where prominent Chinese enterprises and high-level officials come together with WBCSD member companies and external partners. The ceremony will set the tone and objectives for the conference, establishing the stage for collaborative efforts to explore partnerships, form coalitions, and accelerate change at scale. High-Level Roundtable Lunch* This private high-level networking lunch will be hosted by our Chinese partners. More information to be shared soon. Green Transport & EV By highlighting growth opportunities with battery storage, electric vehicles, charging infrastructure and green corridors, this session will: • share best practices on challenges and trends of technology innovation in transport; • highlight key international initiatives that accelerate technology adoption (ZEV-EMI). Contact: Nancy.Gillis@wbcsd.org Deloison@wbcsd.org Renewable Energy Industry To accelerate the availability and adoption of renewable energy and clean technologies , this session will: • share best practices and deepen mutual learning; • highlight key international initiatives that align with these objectives; • share examples of scaling relevant technologies and green fuels, including hydrogen. Contact : Nancy.Gillis@wbcsd.org legoazigo@wbcsd.org Sustainable Agriculture By exploring partnerships, innovative financing models, and cutting-edge technologies to advance sustainable agriculture, this session will: • sharing examples of low-carbon and farmer centric products, practices and business models that create resilient supply chains; • discuss opportunities for Chinese & international collaboration and key enablers to accelerate and support. Contact: Tom.Williams@wbcsd.org

Thematic sessions: International Cooperation on Carbon Accounting and Industrial Decarbonisation 09:00-11:30 Carbon Market & Carbon Finance International Carbon Accounting CBAM and Carbon Reductions Networking Lunch 12:00-14:00 * CP&A High- Level Lunch 12:00-14:00 Closing Ceremony 14:30-17:00 Agenda overview: Friday 5 July 5 Closing Plenary and Ceremony This session will provide a comprehensive summary of the thematic sessions held during The Two Lakes Dialogue, followed by closing remarks on the key outcomes. CP&A High-Level lunch WBCSD’s Corporate Performance and Accountability System (CPAS) outlines critical unlocks between financial markets and green and low carbon transformation at scale. Attendees will participate in a facilitated dialogue on the opportunities and challenges of leadership supporting increased corporate performance and accountability. Carbon market & Carbon Finance This session will aim to strengthen the connection and cooperation between China's and international carbon market platforms, by: • discussing compliance and voluntary carbon markets and mechanisms such as the Chinese and EU ETS; • sharing experiences on how businesses are navigating the associated challenges and opportunities. Contact : Carbone@wbcsd.org International Carbon Accounting This session will enhance the understanding of GHG accounting frameworks as enablers of decarbonization solutions and offer opportunities to: • discuss current challenges and opportunities presented by GHG accounting; • hear from local and international experiences and highlight initiatives on the new frontiers of decarbonization: scope 3 emissions and avoided emissions. Contact: alexander.nick@wbcsd.org Industrial decarbonization This session will focus on understanding how businesses are navigating the three carbon markets/mechanisms: • Emission Trading Systems ( ETS ); • Union Emissions Trading System ( EU ETS ); • EU Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism ( CBAM ). It will delve into the associated challenges and opportunities and provide the latest updates. Contact Nancy.Gillis@wbcsd.org Latest agenda as of June 4 2024.
