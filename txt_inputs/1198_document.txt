SBT4 buildings : project overview The SBT4buildings project provides solutions for companies of the building and construction who want to: • set carbon reduction targets • in line with their own ambitions and Science - based Targets initiative (SBTi) requirements, • working to decarbonize the entire value chain. Project objective Develop a guidance based on a common language and framework for the companies in the sector to align their SBTs along the full value chain. 2019 Objective
