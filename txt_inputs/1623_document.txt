1 WBCSD Forest Solutions Group: L ist of 2024 Key Performance Indicators WBCSD's Forest Solutions Group (FSG) is the global platform where leading business in the forest products sector build and share solutions to sustainable development. FSG’s mission is to grow an inclusive circular bioeconomy that is rooted in thriving working forests. As a sign of shared commitment, members of FSG adhere to Membership Principles & Responsibilities , and report annually on a set of Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to track progress on the commitments made in the Forest Sector SDG Roadmap . This infographic shows a subset of FSG's 2024 KPI results , based on 2023 data. COMMUNITIES 81% of supplier code of conducts had a provision on minimum wage A total of USD $ 83 million was invested in communities WORKING FORESTS Of the 20 million hectares of working forests managed, 98% were certified for sustainable forest management, and 26% were set aside for conservation and restoration purposes BIOECONOMY A total of USD $ 859 million was invested in R&D to bring to market innovative wood fiber products that substitute for less sustainable alternatives CLIMATE 94% of members had time- bound and verified CO2 reduction targets 64% of total energy used came from renewable sources PEOPLE 76% of FSG members reported having a numerical, time- bound target on increasing inclusion and diversity Women made up 27% of the workforce and 25% of senior management WATER 86% of manufacturing operations in water stressed regions had water consumption reduction plans 91% of used water was treated and returned to water sources PROCUREMENT 96% of wood fiber intake was either certified or covered by due diligence and traceability systems CIRCULARITY 74% of process residuals and waste was reused as raw material input or feedstock for other industries 2024 Key Performance Indicator Highlights The KPI results are based on 2023 data from the following FSG member companies: Drax Group, Empresas CMPC, Enviva, Manulife Investment Management, Timberland and Agriculture, International Paper Company, Mondi Group, J M Huber, Congolaise Industrielle des Bois (CIB), Philip Morris International, Sappi Group, SCG Packaging, Smurfit Westrock, Stora Enso, Sumitomo Forestry, The BTG Pactual Timberland Investment Group, The Navigator Company, Weyerhaeuser.
